Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Choose your plan, personalize your box, and subscribe on our website. It’s simple and easy!

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings.

Each box includes a variety of handpicked products based on the plan and personalization choices.
